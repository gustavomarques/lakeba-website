Future Hub

Accelerate your future. Blockchain, Artificial Intelligence, Quantum computing & IOT.

Our Vision

Lakeba’s Future Hub brings together expert partners and international market leaders in technology, legal, education and taxation.

Fused with Lakeba’s proven commercialisation expertise, Future Hub exists to solve complex challenges across all industries, through the development and implementation of blockchain and other emerging technology solutions.

Hear from Our Future Hub partners

Lakeba has a long held partnership with Microsoft, and many of our ventures are developed on the Azure platform. The unique commercial partner program is our access to global markets.

The partnership with Piper Alderman allows Lakeba to help our customers unlock the benefits of blockchain without the legal risk. Piper Alderman is a critically important resource within the Future Hub, and all our customers will have access to their proven legal expertise in the blockchain and cryptocurrency space.

We launched one of Australia’s first Blockchain Commercialisation Accelerator programs with Macquarie University, bringing PhD level students into live commercial Blockchain pilots and exposing them to the cut and thrust of blockchain development.
