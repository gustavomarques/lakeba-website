Our Partners

Collaboration accelerates innovation. In an increasingly connected world, the biggest business wins come from working together.

Lakeba’s success and our place in the world, is a result of the depth and global strength of our partners. Our partnership program is designed to drive creative collaboration and to maximise resources and talent to develop solutions from the ground up.

Our philosophy is to create and foster a profitable, sustainable long term and mutually beneficial relationship between Lakeba and our partners. Harnessing the transformational power of new emerging technologies, we work closely with our partners to solve industry problems, creating solutions, and deploying these on a global scale. Our partners are the world’s leading innovators, and together with our DNA of conceive, create and commercialise, we are recognised for accelerated development on the most forward-looking solutions industry has ever seen. Our partnership program opens doors, and brings Lakeba onto the global stage, inspiring us to create and deploy innovative solutions to change the world for our partner’s customers.

Our global partners

Under their One Commercial Partner model, Lakeba works with Microsoft to identify industry problems, and build highly scalable, cross industry solutions which are promoted through their global network.

This included being invited to join Microsoft at two of the world’s biggest trade shows and Lakeba’s disruptive solutions being acknowledged by Microsoft CEO Satya Nadella at their 2017 global Inspire even.

Fujitsu is a leading provider of customer-focused information technology and communications solutions for the global marketplace. Fujitsu and Lakeba have recently formed a partnership to provide assistance with innovation and commercialization. Developed through our partnership, Fujitsu enables strong scalability, especially in the retail sector.

Lakeba has entered into partnership with IBM as part of the IBM Partner World.

The impact of this strategic partnership is to understand how we can work and collaborate with IBM, particularly in the emerging technology spheres including their Quantum Computing area and AI & Machine Learning capabilities for potential use across our ventures. The relationship offers positive symbiotic value for both partners.

Our Future Hub Blockchain partners

Piper Alderman is a top tier law ﬁrm, that advises corporations on a broad range of legal issues. We will work with Piper Alderman to provide a full service “Blockchain for Business” solution, so that companies can capitalize on the business beneﬁts of this emerging technology.

With more than 40,000 Grant Thornton people, across 130 countries, the ﬁrm is one of the world's leading organisations of independent assurance, tax and advisory ﬁrms focused on making a difference to clients, colleagues and the communities in which we live and work.

Macquarie University and Lakeba Group launch an Australian-ﬁrst Blockchain Commercialisation Accelerator Program

Lakeba and Macquarie University partner to boost the commercial focus on Blockchain technology development at the tertiary level.

Recognised for being one of the ﬁrst innovation companies in Australia to have live commercial blockchain technology pilots in this market and overseas, Lakeba was approached by Professor Tom Smith, Macquarie University head of Department, Applied Finance and Actuarial Studies, to provide practical exposure and active involvement in blockchain developments for students enrolled in the faculty.

“Lakeba’s demonstrated industry leadership in the blockchain development sphere, establishes them as a valued and strategic partner for the Blockchain Commercialisation Accelerator program. We look forward to immersing our students in the cut and thrust of the commercial world of blockchain technology,” said Professor Tom Smith, Faculty of Business and Economics, Macquarie University.
