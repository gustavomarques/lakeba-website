Quixxi Security is an integrated and intelligent platform to secure and manage any mobile app. It allows developers to focus on their core business and helps enterprises to reduce risk and increase revenue.

Three Core Elements (all modules managed through one console):

  • Quxxi Security: Identify vulnerabilities and protect your app, users and revenue.
  • Quixxi Control: Monitor, manage and enhance your app and connect with users.
  • Quixxi Market: Buy and sell certiﬁed SDKs that accelerate app development

Quixxi Connect enables Smart Crypto Payments, where crypto transactions can be sent or received directly to an email address, phone number, or ID, instead of a complex hash address. Complete wallet protection is provided by decentralising shards of the user’s private key. These two unique features make Quixxi Connect a simple and intuitive payments platform, where focus is on breaking down the barriers for mass crypto adoption.

Shelfie is just one example of Lakeba’s innovative strides into software. It’s a cloud-based data analytics platform that automatically analyses retail shelves and recognizes any gaps in products, providing actionable data that maximizes proﬁtability, improves performance and drives revenue.

Shelfie has been revolutionary, and it’s currently been deployed across half a dozen major retail chains in the UK, Europe, the US and Australia. The solution is a world first, as evidenced by the adoption of these global retailers. The solution is embedded with AI, and the software has been from the ground up, partnering with the hardware camera provider AXIS and the Zebra scanning solution.

BAF is a blockchain development that allows any bank to verify in real time that specific documents, like pay slips or tax returns, have been issued by the right source and with the numbers presented by the borrower. This single ledger, decentralised way of capturing information offers significant benefits and is a powerfully transparent way of delivering that much needed single source of truth.

We have recently completed a major pilot in one of Australia’s leading banks, which saw the bank use 100,000 existing pay slips and more than 1000 historical car and home loans, with 100% of validation proved. This is a powerful solution and one that we believe more banks will be adopting as we start to roll out the BAF solution across the broader banking and finance industry, not just in Australia but globally.

Verimoto strives to simplify the process of leasing a vehicle for the consumer and lender by letting the customers provide the required information and images through their smartphone device, via the intuitive Verimoto mobile app. The Verimoto app makes the whole leasing process less cumbersome and faster for the consumer, while reducing costs substantially for leasing companies.

Key benefits of Verimoto:

  • Reduce costs and time by eliminating the need for car inspections, and reduces the risk of fraud through reference automation, report generation and alerts for mismatches of information.
  • Provides a free application with features including; plate optical recognition, owner license data check, VIN scan and reader, ease of use, vehicle check, driver license scan and facial recognition.

Ezidox has developed an award winning platform for collection, veriﬁcation and faster completion of transactions with tier one banks, insurance and other areas of ﬁnancial and professional services. Unlike traditional methods of document collection, we use a secure, compliant and scalable cloud platform, where documents are encrypted and securely stored.

ezidox™, with its intuitive mobile functionality, offers an automated and gamified document upload and indexing process, all through an easy to understand mobile app.

For the banking industry the benefit of ezidox™ is that it provides loan customers with the knowledge to understand exactly what documents are required, by when and in what format, together with the tools to help capture, deliver, process and archive them securely and efficiently, ready for use in the broker’s internal systems. No more wasted time scanning and emailing the documents, customers now have an easy way to capture and send their documents, with the app automatically indexing the documents with the right file name.
