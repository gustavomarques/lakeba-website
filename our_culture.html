We are smart, curious, bold, human-centric, and passionately believe in technology as a powerful enabler regardless of the challenge. We’re egalitarian, enjoy freedom of hierarchy, and embrace an open culture of inclusion and diversity. With a global footprint in four countries, Italy, Australia, India, and the UK, we are an international community of forward thinkers, connected through the desire to bring solutions to market that drive change, and deliver transformational impact to our customers.

As the Fijian island who conceived our name, we have a lush, fertile and prosperous environment. Our strength comes from collaboration and from our smart and passionate teams around the world. With a fast-growing organisation spanning four continents - Europe, Australia, Asia and the US. Together, we are an international community of forward thinkers, connected through the desire to bring innovative solutions to market.

Here at Lakeba we have a valuable ecosystem where everyone can breathe and embrace an open culture of inclusion and diversity in an easy, egalitarian, free of hierarchy space. We pride ourselves on strong collaboration between our groups. Our tribe is many. From sales teams to development engineers, to solutions architects, from accounting to marketing creatives, at Lakeba we are unified by a common desire, a common culture: the culture of growth, development and transformation, in the name of technology innovation.

Curiosity and creative intelligence are two core characteristics we search in people and they are inherent in those who form part of our Lakeba family. We value and are proud of the multicultural fabric of Lakeba, with at least eight different languages being heard across our meeting rooms and our corridors, every day.

We know it’s vital to embrace and open doors to growing young talent - thanks to the ongoing paid internship program with some of our leading education partners including ICMS and Macquarie University. We have reaped rich benefits from embracing new talent into our organisation. Our interns are encouraged to work from the ground up, becoming integral contributors to the success of our business. We have created a forward-thinking community of bold, talented minds capable of responding to the rapid changes occurring in the emerging technology realm.

Together we believe we can grow and make an impact. Come and join us and be part of the Lakeba culture.
