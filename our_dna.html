Our DNA

We believe technology can change the world.

When we bring people together, we create a culture of inclusion and openness. We hope that by building awareness of the importance of diversity and inclusion, and providing a safe place to speak about it, we encourage healthy conversations that create positive, lasting change. We are proud to work together with our community partners that inspire and provide opportunities to those that often need it the most.

We celebrate the uniqueness that every person brings to the team, built upon respect, acceptance and kindness. We know that our success is firmly grounded in the richness of diverse perspectives converging with big, bold ideas. Every person contributes to Canva in their own special way.
